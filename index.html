Quick-Start Guide

How to Play

You'll be trading, crashing, and winning in under 5 minutes. Here's all you need to know.

01
🃏
Setup

Separate Company cards by industry. Each player receives their starting Capital Tracker sheet to record portfolio value throughout the game.

02
📰
The Turn

At the start of every round, draw 1 Market Event card. This changes the economic climate and immediately impacts stock values for all players.

03
⚡
Action Phase

Buy & sell Company cards, navigate market crashes, invest in high-risk startups, or play Competition cards to sabotage your rivals.

04
🏆
Winning

Rounds are fast-paced (10–15 min). The player who manages their portfolio best and accumulates the most wealth wins!
